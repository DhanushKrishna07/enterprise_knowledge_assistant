## 1. Billing & Payments

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**

We accept Visa, Mastercard, and American Express credit/debit cards, as well as net banking and UPI for customers billed in INR. Enterprise customers may also pay via bank transfer with a purchase order.

**Q: Can I get a refund if I cancel my subscription?**

Refunds are available within 7 days of a new subscription or renewal charge if you have not used more than 20% of your storage quota in that period. To request a refund, contact support@nimbuscloud.example with your account email and order ID. Refunds are processed within 5–10 business days.

**Q: How do I update my billing details?**

Log in to your account, navigate to **Settings → Billing**, and click **Update Payment Method**. Changes take effect for the next billing cycle. If your card is declined, you will receive an email notification and have 7 days to update your payment details before your account is downgraded.

**Q: Do you offer annual billing discounts?**

Yes. Annual billing is offered at a discount equivalent to 2 months free compared to the monthly rate. You can switch from monthly to annual billing at any time from **Settings → Billing → Switch to Annual**. The switch takes effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

## 2. Account Management

**Q: How do I reset my password?**

Click **Forgot Password** on the login page, enter your registered email address, and follow the instructions in the email you receive. The reset link is valid for 30 minutes. If you do not receive the email within 5 minutes, check your spam folder or contact support.

**Q: What are the password requirements?**

Passwords must be at least 12 characters long, contain uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters. Passwords expire every 90 days.

**Q: Can I change the email address associated with my account?**

Yes. Go to **Settings → Profile → Email Address** and enter your new email. You will need to verify the new email address before the change takes effect. Your old email will receive a notification of the change.

**Q: How do I add or remove users in my organization account?**

Organization administrators can manage users from the **Admin Console → Users** section. To add a user, click **Invite User** and enter their email address; an invitation will be sent to them. To remove a user, select their name and click **Remove from Organization**. Removed users lose access immediately, and their files remain under the organization's account.

**Q: What happens to my data if I downgrade my plan?**

If your stored data exceeds the limit of the lower plan, your account will be set to read-only until you either upgrade again or delete enough files to fall within the new plan's limit. You will receive a 14-day notice before any downgrade takes effect to give you time to manage your data.

## 3. Technical Support

**Q: What support channels are available?**

| Plan | Support Channels | Response SLA |
|------|-----------------|-------------|
| Free | Help Center (self-serve) | N/A |
| Pro | Email support | 1 business day |
| Business | Email + live chat (business hours) | 4 business hours |
| Enterprise | 24/7 email, chat, and phone | 1 hour (P1), 4 hours (P2) |

**Q: How do I report a bug or technical issue?**

Please contact support via your plan's support channel and include:
- A description of the issue and steps to reproduce it
- Your operating system and browser/client version
- Any error messages you see
- Screenshots or a screen recording if applicable

Our team will triage your report and provide updates as the issue is investigated.

**Q: Is there a status page for NimbusCloud Drive service health?**

Yes. Real-time and historical service status is available at **status.nimbuscloud.example**. You can subscribe to status updates by email or RSS to receive notifications of incidents and maintenance windows.

## 4. Data & Privacy

**Q: Where is my data stored?**

By default, customer data is stored in our primary data centre region. Business and Enterprise customers can select their preferred storage region (e.g., EU, APAC) from the Admin Console at setup.

**Q: Can NimbusCloud staff access my files?**

NimbusCloud staff do not access customer file contents except when explicitly authorized by the customer for the purpose of providing technical support, or when required by applicable law. All such access events are logged and auditable.

**Q: How do I request deletion of my account and data?**

To permanently delete your account and all associated data, go to **Settings → Account → Delete Account**. Data deletion is completed within 30 days of your request, after which recovery is not possible. You will receive an email confirmation when deletion is complete.

## 5. HR & Remote Work Policy

**Q: What is the remote work policy?**

Eligible employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval. Remote employees are provided with an internet and home-office allowance of ₹2,000/month.
